How we work

Quarantune is a free service which creates peronalised unique playlists for each user based on a set of criteria provided to us based on your individual needs. With over 286 million unique active users, Spotify have truly cemented themselves as an everyday essential app accommodating to a broad range of listeners worldwide.

Whether you are looking to bring a range of your favourite artists together for your monthly gym motivation playlist or looking to delve into the depth of Spotifys vast artist selection to unearth some new talent for your ears, we are here to help.

Upon subscribing to our free service, we will ask you to answer a few simples questions relating to your current music prefences, your not so favourite genres and how you intend to use your personalised playlist as this can really determine the choices we make for you in the selective process.

Upon receiving your subscription, we will then get to work creating your playlist based on your unique criteria provided to us and return to you with a personalised playlist for your enjoyment!
